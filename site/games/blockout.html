Blockout: Area Master

Fill the grid with rectangles!

How to Play

Roll 2 dice to get the width and height of a rectangle (e.g. 3 x 4 = 12). Place that rectangle on the 12 x 12 grid. Try to fill as much space as possible without overlapping!

Calculate area (length x width) and fit shapes to maximise grid coverage.
