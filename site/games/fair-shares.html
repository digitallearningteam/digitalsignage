How to Play Fair Shares

Step 1: See the pile

A pile of objects appears. For example, 7 apples.

Step 2: Deal into groups

The objects are shared equally into 3 groups, one at a time, like dealing cards.

Step 3: Spot the remainder

1 apple is left over -- it cannot be shared equally. It stays in the middle and glows!

The equation is: 7 ÷ 3 = 2 remainder 1
