Don't Go Bust!

How to play: Roll a 10-sided die (0-9) each round. Choose whether your number goes in the tens column (worth that many 10p coins) or the ones column (worth that many 1p coins).

Get as close to the target as you can without going over. If 10 or more pennies pile up, they automatically exchange into 10p coins!
