Array Builder
Build rectangles to fill the grid!

How to Play

You are given a target area each round. Pick a rectangle whose area matches the target (e.g. target 12 could be 3 x 4, 2 x 6, etc.), then place it on the grid. Cover as many squares as you can! In 2-player mode, the player who covers more squares wins.
